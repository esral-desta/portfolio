TalkMate

Project Description

TalkMate is designed to connect users globally through engaging discussion rooms. Users can create or join rooms dedicated to specific topics, fostering conversations in their chosen language. The app aims to enhance speaking skills by providing a platform for meaningful interactions, allowing individuals to practice and improve their language abilities in a supportive environment.
